Who We Are

About StandEra

StandEra is a career development platform dedicated to helping students become internship and job ready. We provide real-world project opportunities that help students gain practical experience and build strong portfolios.

Through personalized mock interviews, we evaluate candidates, provide detailed performance analysis, and offer constructive feedback to improve their confidence and interview skills.

StandEra also helps students find internships that match their skills, interests, and career goals. In addition, we assist with creating professional, ATS-friendly resumes and provide training in resume writing, interview techniques, communication, and workplace skills.

By combining practical experience with personalized guidance, StandEra bridges the gap between education and employment, empowering students to confidently begin their professional journey.
